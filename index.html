I am an PhD Student and Graduate Research Assistant under the supervision of Dr. Erman Ayday (SPiDS Lab) from the Department of Computer and Data Sciences at Case Western Reserve University (CWRU). Before that, I received my M.S. degree from the Department of Computer Engineering at Bilkent University, Ankara, Turkey, in 2023, under the supervision of Dr. Ugur Dogrusoz (i-Vis Research Lab). I received my B.S. degree in Computer Science from the COMSATS University Islamabad, Islamabad, Pakistan, in 2021.

My research interests include privacy-enhancing technologies (including big data and genomic privacy) and trust and reputation management. With over 5 years of computer science experience, I have developed a robust skill set in software development, data analysis, and machine learning, allowing me to tackle complex challenges in innovative ways. Throughout my career, I have had the privilege of working on diverse projects, including developing privacy preserving research collaboration framework, privacy preserving distributed data architecture solution, creating a unified complexity management framework for large-scale graph visualizations and more.

I am always eager to learn and grow, and I thrive in environments that challenge me to think outside the box. Let's connect and explore opportunities to collaborate on innovative projects that make a difference!
